Did you try to hum these words while reading? Well everybody does. Music is an inborn delight we all cherish; we just need a little push to realize this passion. At Furtados School of Music we have made it our mission to fine tune this passion in you.

The brand Furtados was established in 1865, with an intent to sell books and musical instruments, gradually moving on to import and distribution of leading brands of musical instruments with 21 retails network all over the country. Furtados is proud to call itself as the first company to import musical instruments after the opening of the Indian economy in the early 1990’s and continues to be the leading provider of musical instruments, accessories and printed music to the music loving community.

Cadence stands for ’punctuation’ in the language of music. A Perfect Cadence means the end of a song and an ‘Imperfect Cadence’ signifies more music coming along. ...read more

True to this definition, our Annual Day music festival is known as Cadence that marks the completion of a year of studies at FSM, and it also represents yet another year of music learning, fun, and celebration. On this day FSM students from all over the country get together to showcase their talent on one stage to create magic through ensemble performances, various genres of music and stage acts and much more. The Cadence festival truly marks the beginning of the musical journey in our students’ lives.

The Band-It Festival is another one of FSM’s brainchild which is created with a vision to promote musical talent across all the schools in the country and bring young talented musicians of the generation under one roof to celebrate music. ...read more

Started in the year 2017, Band-It Festival is the biggest music inter-school competition in India. The festival has already gained a lot of reputation and popularity for its uniqueness to promote young talent in music.

Last year 55 bands participated in the festival, of which 10 bands were selected for the Grand Finale.

For us and everyone involved in the festival, it’s lot more than music, Band It Festival is a celebration. A gathering to celebrate music, laughter, joy and life.
